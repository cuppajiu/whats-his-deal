What's Our Deal?

What's His Deal is a novelty website inspired by a talk Mindy Kaling gave at Made With Code in 2014.

The idea of the game is to insert a conversation you've had with a guy where you had no idea what his deal was, and we'll tell you what it is! It's supposed to be silly and all in good fun.

It was created at Code Fellows by Hannah, Lily, and Charles.

You can (fake) order it here or head back to the main page.
